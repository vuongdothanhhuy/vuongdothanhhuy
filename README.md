# Hi, I’m Huy 👋

I’m a **senior frontend engineer** and a **Roman Catholic catechist**. I care about clarity, responsibility, and truth — in faith ✝️, in teaching 📖, and in software 💻.

## Engineering work 🧑‍💻

I build **enterprise-scale web applications** using **React and Angular with TypeScript**. My focus is on systems that are predictable, maintainable, and easy to reason about.

I work extensively with **state management** using **Redux Toolkit and NgRx**, and integrate data through **REST APIs and GraphQL (Apollo Client)**. When architecture requires it, I also work across the stack with **Node.js and Next.js**.

Most of my professional experience is in **e-commerce**, including checkout flows, performance optimization, analytics, and **GDPR-first system design** for EU clients 🔐. I believe privacy should be a design principle, not an afterthought.

## Teaching & service ⛪

Outside of work, I serve as a **catechist**, dedicating my weekends to teaching children, mentoring youth, and supporting church administration.

Pedagogy shapes how I think. If an idea cannot be explained simply — especially to children — it is not yet truly understood. That principle strongly influences how I write code, documentation, and technical decisions.

## Curiosity & systems thinking 🔍

I enjoy learning how systems work at a fundamental level, across different domains:

Networking, electronics, electricity ⚡, sound engineering 🎚️, photography 📷, telecommunications 📡, and astronomy 🌌.

Different fields, same logic. Once the fundamentals are clear, patterns repeat.

## Guiding principle 🧭

I aim to build software that is **honest**, **understandable**, and **built to serve people well**, not just to ship fast.

> *“If you can’t explain it simply, you don’t understand it well enough.”* — Albert Einstein
